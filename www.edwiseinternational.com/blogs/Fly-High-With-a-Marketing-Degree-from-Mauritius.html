Fly High With a Marketing Degree from Mauritius

With its top-notch education system, stunning natural landscapes, and multicultural setting, Mauritius provides a distinct study abroad experience. Students can benefit from a varied cultural background, budget-friendly tuition fees, and the chance to discover beautiful beaches and tropical landscapes.

In Mauritius, Marketing programs generally emphasize on providing students with abilities in consumer behavior, branding, market research, and digital marketing, frequently including a global business perspective. Undegraduate programs in Marketing typically take 3-4 years to complete, whereas postgraduate programs generally span 1-2 years, based on whether they are pursued full-time or part-time.

Why study Marketing in Mauritius?

Studying Marketing in Mauritius is a popular choice for the following reasons:

  • Top-tier Education: Mauritius is home to prominent universities and business schools that offer internationally acknowledged marketing programs.
  • Secure and Steady: The country is renowned for its safety and political stability, thereby, making it an ideal place to learn..
  • Fluency in English and French: Bilingual education improves communication abilities, which are essential for jobs in international marketing.
  • Multicultural Setting: The varied population enables students to study marketing in a multicultural atmosphere, equipping them for worldwide careers.
  • Central Location: Mauritius, located as an access point between Africa and Asia, provides a distinctive fusion of cultural viewpoints, that is beneficial for marketing education.
  • Developing Economy:With a strong emphasis on finance, tourism, and technology, Mauritius is a fast-growing economy that offers an abundance of real-world marketing chances.
  • Tourism Sector: A robust tourism industry provides real-world insights into destination marketing and hospitality management.
  • Booming Business Hotspot: Mauritius is a business-friendly atmosphere with a number of international firms, providing networking and internship possibilities.

Best Marketing specializations to pursue in Mauritius

The best Marketing specializations for students to pursue in Mauritius are:

  • Data Analytics and Marketing Insights
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Event Management and Marketing
  • Marketing Strategy and Planning
  • Digital Marketing
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • Brand Management
  • Public Relations and Corporate Communications
  • Content Marketing

What’s the cost of studying Marketing in Mauritius?

Based on the institution, degree of study, and selected program, the cost of studying Marketing in Mauritius differs. For undergraduate degrees, public universities such as the University of Mauritius charge approximately between MUR 50,000-MUR 100,000 in tuition annually. On the contrary, higher tuition fees, usually between MUR 150,000-MUR 400,000 annually may be charged by private universities and global institutions operating in Mauritius. Postgraduate programs can also differ, usually ranging from MUR 100,000-MUR 300,000 per year. These expenses typically entail only tuition fees, and students should also take into account supplementary costs like books, housing, and individual living expenses, which can substantially increase the total cost of studying.

Eligibility Criteria to study Marketing in Mauritius

The eligibility criteria to pursue Marketing in Mauritius are:

  • For undergraduate programs, the student needs to hold a high school diploma or comparable. For postgraduate programs, the student needs to hold a Bachelor’s degree in an associated field.
  • He/she should have obtained minimum grades set by the institution for pertinent subjects like English and Mathematics.
  • The student needs to submit official academic transcripts from all education institutions previously attended.
  • Non-native English speakers need to prove their fluency in English through appearing for English language proficiency tests such as the IELTS or TOEFL.
  • For postgraduate programs, the student needs to possess pertinent work experience.
  • Prior knowledge or coursework in business studies or economics may be demanded by certain institutions.
  • Submission of a personal statement.
  • Recommendation letters.
  • Candidates may be needed to pass an entrance exam as required by certain universities.
  • As part of the selection procedure, an interview may be required by certain programs.
  • The student needs to provide proof of adequate finances to pay for tuition fees and living costs.
  • Submission of a filled-out application form.
  • Current passport and student visa for Mauritius.
